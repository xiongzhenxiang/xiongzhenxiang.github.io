# CookieBear Adventure Loop

## Style Prompt

An original, warm, hand-painted 2D side-scrolling fantasy world for a game developer. The frame should feel playful and expansive, with layered mountains, enchanted foliage, soft clouds, a friendly chibi adventurer, and restrained magical particles. Motion is calm, buoyant, and perfectly cyclical rather than cinematic or dramatic.

## Colors

- Sky blue `#55B9EE` — open atmosphere
- Forest green `#2E6C45` — environmental depth
- Sunlit gold `#F6D36A` — warmth and highlights
- Crystal cyan `#53D7FF` — magical accent
- Deep pine `#102E2B` — shadows and grounding

## Typography

No on-screen typography. The scene communicates only through environment and character motion.

## Motion

- Six-second seamless cycle
- Slow sine-eased environmental drift
- Gentle character idle bob with a stable ground contact point
- Crystal glow and fireflies pulse on exact divisors of the master duration
- No entrances, exits, cuts, or one-way motion that would expose the loop seam

## What NOT to Do

- No copied game maps, characters, logos, or interface elements
- No text, captions, HUD, or watermark
- No abrupt camera movement or bounce easing
- No dark neon-tech treatment
- No motion that fails to return to its exact starting state at six seconds
